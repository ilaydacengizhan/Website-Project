OUR TEAMS

MINI TEAM

Our mini team is an energetic volleyball team consisting of young talents born in 2013 and 2014. This team offers athletes the opportunity to learn the basics of volleyball and enjoy the game. Our young volleyball players continue to discover and develop their potential while having fun in a loving atmosphere

MIDI TEAM

Our Midi Team brings together young talents who are ready for the excitement and competition of volleyball at the middle school level. The Midi Team aims to maximize the potential of young athletes through disciplined training and teamwork

STAR TEAM

Our Star Team is a volleyball family that includes young talents born in 2007, 2008 and 2009. Our Stars prioritize not only volleyball skills, but also team spirit, leadership skills and character development
